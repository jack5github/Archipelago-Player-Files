# World Items

This document lists items that are commonly observed in various Archipelago worlds, in order to discover connections between them and add these connections into player files as `item_links` entries. Codified item group names are written as `code blocks`, while unimplemented groups are written in *italics*.

For more information on the `item_links` option, refer to **[Common Properties](./common-props.md#item_links)**.

## Archipeladoku

- `Blocks` - Reveals blocks of sudoku cells that can then be solved. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Items` - Gives items that can be used to solve sudoku cells.
- `Traps` - Gives ~1 minute debuffs that hinder one's ability to solve sudoku cells.

## Balatro

- *Decks* - Gives decks of cards that represent various challenges. (Directly related to game completion.)
- *Stakes* - Gives stakes that represent steadily upgrading challenges applied on top of decks. (Unused by Jack5.)
- Unlock purchasable items that can be used to improve one's score in game rounds:
  - *Joker Bundles*
  - *Tarot Cards*
  - *Planet Cards*
  - *Spectral Cards*
  - *Vouchers*
  - *Booster Packs*

## ChecksMate

- `Progressive AI Intelligence Malus` - Reduces the ELO rating of the AI player. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Chessmen` - Gives chess pieces that can be used to play chess. (Directly related to game completion.)

## Civilization V

- *Eras* - Unlocks eras, immediately giving one their benefits and allowing one to research new technologies using Science. (Directly related to game completion\.)
- *Technologies* - Gives technologies that unlock new units and buildings. (Directly related to game completion.)
- *Policies* - Unlocks policy trees, allowing one to research new policies using Culture.

## Dracomino

- *Shapes* - Gives Tetris pieces that are added to the bag, allowing one to play Tetris for longer. (Directly related to game completion.)
- *Rotations* - Unlocks the ability to rotate Tetris pieces. (Directly related to game completion.)
- *Hold Slots* - Unlocks the ability to hold Tetris pieces.

## Minecraft (Fabric)

- `Ruby` - Advances one closer to game completion. (Unused by Jack5.)
- Unlocks new abilities:
  - `Swim`
  - `Sprint`
  - `Jump`
  - `Chests & Barrels`
  - `Sleeping`
  - `Wither Summoning`
  - `Villager Trading`
  - `Piglin Bartering`
  - `Brewing`
  - `Enchanting`
  - `Smithing`
  - `Other Crafting Stations`
  - *Recipes*
- `Progressive Tools` - Allows the use of stronger tools. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Progressive Weapons` - Allows the use of stronger weapons. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Progressive Smelting` - Allows placing stronger materials into a furnace. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Progressive Armor` - Allows the use of stronger armour.
- *Traps* - Gives time-limited debuffs that hinder one's ability to play Minecraft.

## OpenRCT2

- *Rules* - Lifts restrictions applied to the early game. (Directly related to game completion.)
- *Rides* - Unlocks rides that can be added to one's park. (Directly related to game completion.)
- *Stalls* - Unlocks stalls that can be added to one's park.
- *Cash* - Gives cash that can be used to buy rides and stalls, pay off loans and advertise one's park.
- *Guests* - Creates guests that immediately enter one's park.
- *Traps* - Gives time-limited debuffs that hinder one's ability to play RollerCoaster Tycoon 2.

## PEAK

- `Progressive Stamina Bar` - Gives 25% more stamina. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Progressive Endurance` - Reduces one's consumption of stamina. (Directly related to game completion.)
- *Items* - Spawns items in one's vicinity that, when picked up, often release another item.
- *Traps* - Gives time-limited debuffs that hinder one's ability to play PEAK.

## The Witness

- `Symbols` - Unlocks puzzles that use a specific type of symbol.
- `Lasers` - Activates lasers that gradually unlock the puzzle at the top of the mountain. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Useful` - Gives time-limited buffs that improve one's ability to play The Witness.
- `Traps` - Gives time-limited debuffs that hinder one's ability to play The Witness.

## Yacht Dice

- `Dice Fragment` - Gives a dice fragment that, when combined with other dice fragments, can unlock additional dice. (Directly related to game completion.)
- `Roll Fragment` - Gives a roll fragment that, when combined with other roll fragments, can unlock additional rolls per turn. (Directly related to game completion.)
